❓ Frequently Asked Questions Progressive Disclosure

How do I follow my favorite team?
Click the ⭐ star icon on any team's match card or player profile page. Your favorites are saved locally and will appear first in your feed. You can manage them from Settings.
How does the Fantasy Predictor work?
Before each match, you can predict the winner, the winning score, and the Man of the Match. Points are awarded based on accuracy. Check the leaderboard to see your ranking!
How do I enable Dark Mode?
Click the moon icon (🌙) in the navbar, or press the D key on your keyboard. Your preference is saved automatically. You can also change it from the Settings page.
Are live scores real-time?
Yes! Our live scores update ball-by-ball with minimal delay. Look for the green "LIVE" badge on match cards. Commentary updates appear instantly in the match detail view.
How do I use keyboard shortcuts?
Press ? to see all shortcuts. Key shortcuts: D = Toggle dark mode, / = Focus search, H = Go to home page. Shortcuts work from any page.
How do I change accessibility settings?
Go to Settings & Accessibility to change font size, contrast mode, reduce animations, and more. We follow WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines.
